About Press to Impress

Press to Impress is a local ironing service, set up and run by myself - Mandy Allen.

My aim is to make everyday life a little easier for my customers by taking a repetative household chore off their hands.

With free pick up & delivery (within 3 miles of Thurcroft) and a quick turnaround time you can give yourself time to do what you enjoy.

Book a pick up slot today on my facebook page! I can also be contacted by phone or email. All of this information can be found in the contact section.

Your ironing will be returned hanging and/or folded and covered.
